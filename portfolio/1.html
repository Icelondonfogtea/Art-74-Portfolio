This is a glitch made by two different methods. They modify the picture in the form of code and change the picture into an audio file. We can see the effect of these two methods. From top to bottom, the first one is the original picture, the second one is the code modification, and the third one is the audio modification by using Audacity. Then I found that the effect of glitch by converting files into txt files is very different from that of glitch by using Audacity. The effect of glitch is more inclined to the change of the overall color of the photo by converting the photo into a TXT file. By modifying the amplitude, the photo can even present a feeling similar to that of a movie film. Using Audacity to glitch a photo is a way similar to audio modification, which converts the photo data into audio shape for modification. The effect obtained in this way usually has a lot of graininess, similar to the wave fluctuation of audio, but the tone of the whole photo will not change much unless the modification range is very large As if a part of the whole picture had been deleted.
